Announcing Spots 2.0

Spots 2.0 is a signifanct upgrade to the popular Spots cryptocurrency. Spots 2.0 offers enhanced functionality; with several new features outlined below. The most important feature isn't a part of the program. It's a focus on building a community! We need you to get involved and make a difference!

Spots 2.0 Features

  • On June 1st, 2014 @ 00:00:00 GMT Spots 2.0 will hard fork from Spots v1.
  • After Hard Fork - Difficulty adjusted using Kimoto's Gravity Well (KGW).
  • After Hard Fork - Hashing algorithm changed to Scrypt-N for ASIC resistance.
  • Unchanged - 48 SPT Block Reward.
  • Unchanged - 70 Second Block Target.
  • Unchanged - Difficulty Retarget Every Block.
